Grilled Cheese

Description

This is the best grilled cheese recipe.


Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Dijon mustard
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on the outside of the bread.
  2. Add grated cheese.
  3. Choose the right skillet.
  4. Go low and slow.
  5. Cover the pan.
  6. Adjust the heat as needed.
